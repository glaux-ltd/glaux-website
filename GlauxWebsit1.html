About GLAUX

Intelligent guidance
for every buyer

Buying a used car is one of the most significant financial decisions most people make. Yet the process remains opaque, stressful, and prone to costly mistakes.

GLAUX brings clarity to the process. Our platform combines public data, market intelligence, and AI to help New Zealand buyers make informed, confident decisions.

Named after the ancient owl of Athena, GLAUX embodies the wisdom of seeing clearly before you act.

01

Vehicle Intelligence

Access comprehensive vehicle history, registration, and ownership data in one place — before you make an offer.

02

Market Pricing

Understand the true market value of any vehicle with real-time pricing benchmarks from the New Zealand used car market.

03

Risk Assessment

Identify hidden risks — from financial encumbrances to odometer discrepancies — with a clear, easy-to-read report.
